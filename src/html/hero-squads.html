Hero Squads

Hero Squads are a group of people working closely and focused together on a common goal. Hero Squads are formed during Mission Briefing ceremony. It is highly encouraged that Engineers are shuffled when a new Hero Squad is formed, based on the personal interest, in order to maximize the values of challenge and learning.

Hero Squads should be motivated to bring their best self to fullfil a Mission and be trusted that will self-organize to make this happen. Self-organization and safety will let Guest Leadership to emerge for the duration of the Mission.

A Hero Squad is dismissed when their Mission is over, stalled or aborted. Their human capacity may grow or shrink based on the state of progress, in case a Mission may need to span across multiple cycles.
